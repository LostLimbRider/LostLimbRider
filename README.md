# Who We Are

Lost Limb Riders is a nonprofit organization built by amputees, for amputees.

Our mission is simple:

Help people rediscover independence, confidence, purpose, and the freedom that comes with getting back on two wheels—and back into life.

We believe an amputation is not the end of someone's story.

It's the beginning of a new chapter.
